Caliz Music Studio, located at Angeles City in Pampanga, serves as a space for worry-free jamming, It serves as a musical santuary for musicians and bands. A place for singers and songwriters to enjoy music. The challenge was to build the Caliz Music experience online through their website and brand elements.

A design that's responsive for mobile and desktop, it looked to communicate Caliz Music Studios' mission without compromising the usability and experience. The design wanted to capture the attention of musicians and band through key visual elements, copy that is straightforward but light in tone, has good typography, and a convenient, intuitive, and functional user journey.
