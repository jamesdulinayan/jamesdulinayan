Kaleidoscope
An Art for Heart

By buying or commissioning an artwork, part of the proceeds will go directly to the HAUwak Kamay Sabay nating Abutin ang Pangarap na Edukasyon Donation Drive that aims to help the Angelites who cannot provide internet access to reach education. With this, we show support to the student-artists and promote their local artworks as we simultaneously create ripples of hope on a good cause. My role for this project is to create publication materials such as an animated cover to boosts engagements.
