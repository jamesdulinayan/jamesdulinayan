enuf-proj

Product Design

A mockup designs for enuf-proj, a skin care line. A future-forward cosmetics that believes in beauty the way hue want it. Being mindful about what is happening in the world also pushed the product to think more beyond graphic design and copy, and reflect it to the packaging. The commitment to sustainability showed in the choice of reusable glass amber bottles, biodegradable & plant-based gel cleanser, and labels produced.

A great visual identity isn't about design; it's about creating a better brand experience. That's the main goal for this design.
