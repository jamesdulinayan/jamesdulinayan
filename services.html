A designer and developer devoted in crafting works that are focused on simplicity and purpose, creating art that people will enjoy and appreciate without compromising quality. I strive to propel business, culture, and people forward through strategic thinking and good projects.

My goal is to work with forward-thinking brands or companies who are not afraid to experiment or push boundaries in creativity & business.

ART FOR GOOD

The world of digital design has grown at an extremely rapid rate over the last 10 years and my aim has been to evolve with it by learning and gaining new skills every day. Let's create work that positively impacts our stakeholders: team, clients, community, and most importantly, planet.

—

A: Good Initiatives (Team): Allocating hours for collaborative impact work.

B: Brand Advisory (Clients): Advising clients on future-forward practices.

C: Creative Economy (Community): Expanding creative network to the community.

D: Sustainability (Planet): Promoting ecological footprint through art.

CREATIVE PROCESS

1: Ideate: Generate large number of ideas that potentially inspire newer and better ideas, which can then filter down into the best, most practical, and innovative ones.

2: Prototype: Structured design process that will include iterations to help us get the final approved designs. Once we have the final design we will ensure that everything works and looks great.

3: Test: Launch the design and evaluate how it performs.

4: Evaluate: Gather consumers' feedbacks and take-aways. Bridging the gap between design feedbacks and tecnhical concerns creates a better product.
